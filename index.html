Why Choose CureTrack?

Automated medication tracking that integrates with your calendar and keeps complete treatment history.

Smart Reminders

Get alerts for start dates, end dates, dose switches, expiration dates, next cure dates, and low stock warnings - all automatically synced to your Google Calendar.

Complete History

Track every treatment cycle with detailed history in Notion. Start, pause, restart - every medication journey is recorded and traceable.

No Duplicates

PostgreSQL prevents duplicate alerts, ensuring you only get the reminders you need, when you need them.

Auto-Updates

Automatically saves remaining stock and resets cycles when treatments end. Your data stays current without manual intervention.

Calendar Integration

Seamless Google Calendar integration ensures you never miss critical medication events with real-time reminders.

Organized Data

Keep supplements and treatment history organized in Notion databases with calculated fields for days elapsed and stock remaining.

How It Works

Simple setup, powerful automation

1

Connect Your Tools

Set up integrations with Google Calendar, Notion, and PostgreSQL through n8n workflows.

2

Import Your Data

Add your supplements and medications to the Notion database with start dates, dosages, and treatment cycles.

3

Automate Everything

Let CureTrack handle the rest - daily runs check dates, create reminders, and keep your treatment history updated.
